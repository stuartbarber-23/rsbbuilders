About Us

Our aim is to provide high quality service from the initial consultation to beyond the completed project.

We are well aware of the importance of providing and maintaining assurance to our customers of our professionalism as well as providing up to date knowledge of legislation to all aspects of the construction industry.

Our company was first established in 1984. We have maintained a good relationship with our former customers who have consistently provided recommendations of our professionalism and workmanship to their friends. This in turn has helped our new customers to be comfortable at our first introduction, knowing we are reliable and our word can be trusted.

A wealth of diversity in our company has been gained over many years of trading and this includes the construction of: Traditional House Build, Extensions, Alterations, Loft Conversions, Modern and traditional Barn Conversions, Conservatories, Brickwork, All aspects of Carpentry, specialising in bespoke roof construction and Hard Landscaping projects. All work previously undertaken has been in the Surrey and Sussex areas.

All work to date has come from recommendation from former customers but now we wish to provide an opportunity for other potential customers to savour our expertise through our web page. Our portfolio gives a small guide to the diversity of projects we have undertaken. It also includes all the associated trades involved such as: Plumbing, heating and Electrical Services to residential properties.

We would like to hope we can provide a greater knowledge through our experiences of the industry and bring this to you. This we are sure will help you with your own initial consideration towards your project.

Any enquiry is welcome whether it is large or small to your proposed project. Any enquiry you make is without obligation to you and is viewed by us as a free service.

Our work is undertaken by experienced members of our company and is guaranteed.

R.S. Barber
